About the event via FAQs





  • What is AME-2K16?

    Advances in Mechanical Engineering - 2K16 (AME-2K16) is a "Technical Paper Presentation" competition hosted by the Mechanical department of Vidya Vikas Institute Engineering and Technology, Mysuru. The sole intention of this competition is to familiarise and to introduce the contestantsto the world of research.

  • What are the rules and regulations of the competiton?

    Candidates in a group of 2 or 3 have to pick a topic from the list. They have to research and draft a paper regarding the topic they choose and submit it to us at ame.mech.vviet@gmail.com. On the day of presentation, the candidates have to assemble in the venue (that will be mailed to each team) and present their papers in the best spirit. It is very important to note that the paper has to be backed up with a Power Point Presentation and the Presentation has to be delivered in a time scrutiny of 6+2 seconds. 6 seconds is for presentation and rest 2 seconds is for the Q&A session.
